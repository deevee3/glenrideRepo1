### 1. From "nonprofit" to "productive enterprise"
Stop apologizing for ambition. You're building an empire—just a public one.

Foundations spend. Holding companies invest. Use their language.

### 3. From "zero exits" to "community exits"
You DO have exits—just to community ownership, not venture capital.

### 4. From "programs" to "subsidiaries"
Programs are expenses. Subsidiaries are assets. Frame accordingly.

### 5. From defensive to generative
Stop explaining what you're NOT. Focus on what you're BUILDING.
